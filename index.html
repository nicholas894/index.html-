Privacy Policy - Clip Generator

Last updated: July 2026

Clip Generator is a personal-use tool that automatically creates short-form video clips from gaming/streaming content and posts them to a single TikTok account that I own and operate. This policy explains what data the app touches and how it is handled.

What this app does

The app downloads publicly available stream/video content, generates short video clips with AI-created captions and titles, and posts those clips to my own TikTok account using TikTok's Content Posting API.

What data is accessed

  • A TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token, used solely to post videos to my own connected TikTok account.
  • Video files and generated captions/titles, which are processed and stored locally on my own computer.

What this app does NOT do

  • It does not collect, access, or store data belonging to any other TikTok user or account.
  • It does not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.
  • It does not use TikTok data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than posting content to my own account.

Data storage and retention

All video files, tokens, and generated content are stored locally on my own computer, not on any third-party server. Tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the app and can be revoked at any time through TikTok's account settings.

Data sharing

No data collected or generated by this app is sold, rented, or shared with any third party for any purpose.
